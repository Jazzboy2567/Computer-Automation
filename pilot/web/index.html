Before you start

Pilot drives your browser using your own logged-in sessions and acts on your behalf.

  • Automated access may violate some sites' Terms of Service and could put your accounts at risk of restriction.
  • You are responsible for which sites you target and what you do there.
  • Pilot never handles passwords — you log in manually. It ships no anti-bot evasion and will pause for you on a CAPTCHA or bot check rather than attempt to solve it.
  • risk actions (spending money, submitting, deleting, changing settings) pause for your approval by default.
